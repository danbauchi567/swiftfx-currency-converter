About SwiftFX

SwiftFX is a modern, open-source currency converter designed for speed, simplicity, and accuracy. It uses live exchange rates from reliable APIs and supports over 180 currencies worldwide.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy — no personal data is stored. All exchange rate requests are handled anonymously through free public APIs. You can clear any locally saved rates at any time in your browser’s local storage.
